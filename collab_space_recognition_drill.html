THE CoL LAB Field Hockey Space Recognition

Player Prep Drill

Space
Recognition

Three possession situations. Identify where the space is. Decide where to move before the ball arrives.

Scenario 1 of 3

Scenario 01

Your team has the ball.
The court is small.

The situation

Your team wins possession in the middle third. Five players are within 10 yards of the ball. Both sideboard zones are empty. The opposition press is collapsing toward the ball.

Where is the space, and where should you move right now?

Scenario 02

You are off the ball.
The back has space.

The situation

Your back has the ball on the left boards. The opposition has three players pressing the left side. The entire right side of the court is open. You are a midfielder standing in the center.

What do you do to help your back find the space?

Scenario 03

Your team is in possession.
The press has adjusted.

The situation

The opposition press has shifted to cover the right side where the ball is. The left side of the court is now completely open. You are a forward near the attacking circle, currently marked on the right.

Where is the space, and what is your job right now?
