Before you start

In indoor field hockey, the court is small and the press comes fast. The teams that handle pressure well are not just more skilled — they read space earlier. They know where to move before the ball arrives.

This drill gives you three game situations. In each one, your team has possession and the opposition is pressing. Your job is to find the space and decide where to move. There is no ball coming yet. You are making the read first.

Situation 01

The court has collapsed.
The boards are empty.

Your team (lime) has possession. Opposition (red) is pressing the ball. Both sideboard zones are empty.

What is happening

Your team just won possession in the middle third. Five of your players are within ten yards of the ball. The opposition has three players pressing tight around the ball carrier. Both sideboards are completely open. You are the player marked YOU on the diagram.

👁

Your job: Read where the space is and decide where to move before the ball comes to you. The press is collapsing to the center. What does that tell you?

Where should you move right now?

Situation 02

Your back is trapped.
The press has overloaded one side.

Your back (lime, bottom left) has the ball. Three opposition players are pressing the left side. The right side is open.

What is happening

Your back has the ball on the left boards and is under pressure from three opposition players. Your other teammates are also on the left side. The entire right side of the court is open. You are the player marked YOU — a midfielder standing in the center. Your back has no clean outlet right now.

👁

Your job: Your back needs an outlet. The press has committed three players to the left. What does that tell you about where to move?

What do you do to help your back find the space?

Situation 03

The press has shifted.
One side is now unguarded.

The opposition (red) has shifted all four players to the right side. The entire left side of the court is unguarded.

What is happening

Your team has possession on the right side. The opposition has shifted all four players to press that side. The entire left side of the court is unguarded. You are a forward marked on the right near the circle. Your marker is right next to you.

👁

Your job: The press has left a huge gap on the left. If your team can switch the ball to that side, the press is beaten. What do you need to do to make that switch happen?

Where is the space and what is your job right now?
