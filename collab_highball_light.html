Key rules to know first
Dangerous ball: causes legitimate evasive action by players.
General standard: below knee height is generally not dangerous.
Intentional lift: only legal on a shot at goal, inside the circle.
Remember: there is no "high ball" call in field hockey, only a dangerous ball call.
