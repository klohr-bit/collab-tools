The key takeaway
Great players do not react faster — they read earlier. Check your defender before the ball arrives. Feet tell you their weight. Hips tell you their direction. Stick tells you what they are protecting. Read all three and your decision is already made by the time the ball gets to you.
Take this to practice
Pick one thing to focus on in your next training session: scanning your defender before you receive. Every time the ball is traveling to you, your eyes go to the defender first. Do it for one full practice and notice how much earlier your decisions come.
