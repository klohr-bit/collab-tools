The key takeaway
There is no high ball call in field hockey — only a dangerous ball call. The question is never how high the ball went. The question is always: did someone have to get out of the way? Train your players to ask that question and you will have fewer arguments and better decisions on both sides of the ball.
Take this to practice
Run a short scrimmage and call out "high ball situation" when the ball lifts. Pause play and ask your players to make the call before you give it. Use the three scenarios from this drill. See how quickly they start applying the standard on their own.
