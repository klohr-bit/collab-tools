Zone
X Press
The X Press uses a V-shape at the front with two forwards and a mid forming a triangle that blocks the center of the court. The backs sit behind to intercept anything that gets through. The center is locked. The boards are the outlet.
X Press — center locked, boards available
Locked — do not enter
Key — use the boards
Key — do this
Transfer quickly to the boards. Use shape changes and overloads on the side. High ball speed to move before they close.
Lock — avoid this
Trying to force the ball through the center V. That is exactly where they want it to go.

German Press
The German Press channels play to one side — specifically pushing you toward the left boards. A diagonal line of defenders pressures your forehand and blocks the left side. Do not go left. Go right. This was Team USA's press of choice in the 2024-25 Indoor season.
German Press — diagonal line, go right
Key — do this
Go right. Use the right side boards and the open space. Deceptive passes off the left foot of the pressing forward. Change shape to create overloads on the strong side.
Lock — avoid this
Going left toward the boards. That is where they are channeling you. It is a trap.

Zone
Arrow Press
The Arrow Press uses a forward point — one forward pressing hard at the top of an arrow shape — with the other defenders spreading behind them. The center channel is locked hard. Play to the outside, then look to exploit space behind the press.
Arrow Press — center locked, play wide
Key — do this
Play wide to the outside. Use the boards. Move into the Arrow pocket behind the point forward. Change shape to create overloads.
Lock — avoid this
Playing into the center channel where the arrow tip is waiting. That is where the pressure is concentrated.

Man to Man
Clark M2M
Named after Caitlin Clark's aggressive man-to-man defense. Each defender has one job: follow their assigned player everywhere and prevent them from receiving the ball. The Key is movement. Create space through leading runs and space balls.
Clark M2M — every player is marked, move to unlock
Your team
Assigned defender
Key — do this
Move constantly. Lead into space and make your defender follow. Space balls to teammates cutting. Midfield movement to clear space for forwards. Use your marker against the press.
Lock — avoid this
Standing still and waiting for the ball. In M2M if you do not move, your defender does not move — and the space never opens.
